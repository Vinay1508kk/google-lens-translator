## 🌟 Overview
**Critic Lens** is a powerful, privacy-conscious, and visually stunning Streamlit app that replicates and extends the core functionality of Google Lens — with a critical twist: it doesn’t just extract text; it **analyzes, translates, speaks, and contextualizes** it.

Built for students, travelers, researchers, and curious minds, Critic Lens turns any image, screenshot, or PDF into actionable insight — in **12+ languages**.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📸 **Multi-source input**: Upload images, PDFs, or use your camera  
- 🔍 **Advanced OCR**: Powered by EasyOCR (supports English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, French, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese)  
- 🌐 **Smart translation**: Auto-detect source language + translate to your choice  
- 🔊 **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Hear translations spoken aloud  
- 📋 **One-click copy**: Copy extracted or translated text instantly  
- 🔎 **Context search**: Search detected text on Google with one click  
- 👁️ **Google Lens integration**: Analyze your image visually via Google Lens  
- 🌓 **Immersive dark UI**: Unique indigo-violet-cyan theme inspired by editorial intelligence  
- 📦 **PDF support**: Extract text from multi-page documents
